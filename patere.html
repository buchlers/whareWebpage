He tītī e rere

Composed by Michelle Lee and James Webster, 2008. This patere tells of places within the Te Whitianga-ā-Hei area, shared by Joe Davis. Translation by Brandon Amoamo.

Ka tū ake au…Ka tū ake au…i te tihi o moutere Ohinau e…i! Here Istand…Here I stand on the peak of Ohinau island! He tītiī e rere, he tītī e ruku…i nga tai o Whitianga a Kupe! Like themutton bird I fly...like the mutton bird I dive…into the swell ofWhitianga’s tides! Kia tere te haere ki te maunga tū tonu…te maunga Tāhanga, In bird formI travel, swiftly to the ancient mount of Tāhanga, Pakawera, toketoke, taketake, ngā toki o ngā tūpuna. To motherlode offinest basalt, source of exquisite anscestral adzes, tempered, craftedand expertly wrought, chipped and chinked from aboriginal stone. Te whatu o Poutini e… i My bird recalls, the eye of monstrous Poutini,progenator of Greenstone who fled, who fled… Arā… Riporipo te tai…tumutumu ngā wai, Over languorous swirls ofocean I soar…over corrugated rhythms of tide and sand, I runga onepū a Wharekaho… Upon the beach at Wharekaho… Ōhuka ki taitimu, taipare o Taputapuātea, As tide ebbs at Ōhuka, thetide peaks at Taputapuātea, Ka rērere atu rā ki Whangamāroro…e tū mai ana ko Te Ana… My flightcarries me onto Whangamāroro, where there stands Te Ana… Ka tae maumahara k Wai-toto-rā…Aue! My thoughts return to thosebloodier days when water ran red at Waitotorā…Alas! Ka heke atu au i te taumata ko Motukū te puke, In flight I descend to thesummit of Motukū, Māringi noa puawai o te pōhutukawa…e…i… Where the delicate redbloom of pohutukawa spills like blood into the hallowed earth of mymemories of this place…alas… Ka tāhuri nei au te wāhi Puahape…Tahura te tūpuna! As I fly, I turn nowto Puahape…the territory of that eponymous grand sire, Tahura! He Wharetangata, he whare mātauranga…Tū tonu i te pō, tū tonu i teao… Further I glide, over the birthing grounds of the people, to thegrounds of their minds…Ever present by night, ever present by day… I runga parirau a Hei… Hei Hā! On the blessed wings of Hei I swift,indeed, I do!
